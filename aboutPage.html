Who We Are

Minerva Art Gallery is a small team of dedicated artists and art connisuers who wish to spread the talent of others. Our mission is to spread the word about the countless talented individuals who go un-noticed. Art deserves to be shared and consumed. Art is for the people.

How to Submit Artwork

Submitting work is easy and rather straight forward. Send your portfolio via email to in a PDF form. Our Team will carefully review your submission and will contact you further if they wish for your work to be displayed.
